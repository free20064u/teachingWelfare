About Our Association

A community-driven organization dedicated to the well-being and professional growth of educators.

Our Aims and Objectives

To seek the interest and welfare of the teaching staff of W.B.M. Zion Senior High School.
To promote solidarity and cooperation among members of staff.
To provide a means for the expression oI the collective opinion of teachers in matters regarding the welfare of the teacher and to participate in decision making.
To create a forum for members to socialize,share ideas and experiences
To take such measures as may be deemed necessary for the progress of the association including.Investment in Fixed deposits. Treasury bill and othey financial instruments.
Granting loans to members through a special purpose vehicle to be established by the Executive committee
The provision in 2(e) above is subject to general assembly approval.
To establish and strengthen relations with teaching staff welfare associations in other Senior High Schools
