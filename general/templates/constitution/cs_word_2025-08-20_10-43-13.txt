ARTICLE 1I: DISSOLUTION
a. The association shall not be dissolved and its funds divided except with the consent of not less than two-thirds (2/3) of members of the association voting at a meeting specially summoned for this purpose
b. In the event of the association being dissolved as provided in 11(a) above.all debts and liabilies legally incurred on behalf of the association shall befully settled and the remalning funds divided up among the membership in equal proportions.
